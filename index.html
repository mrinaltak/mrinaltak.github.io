About me

I am currently a Senior Analyst at Goldman Sachs, working in the Compliance division. My research interests are in Algorithmic Game Theory, Data Mining, applied machine learning, particularly deep learning methods for NLP.

I graduated from IIT Guwahati in 2017 with a major in Computer Science. My bachelor thesis was on designing payment mechanism in Knapsack Auctions, supervised by Prof. G. Sajith.
